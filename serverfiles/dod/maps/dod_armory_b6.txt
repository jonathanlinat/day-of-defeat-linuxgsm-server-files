1944, Germany

In the heart of Germany, a strategic armory becomes the focal point of an intense skirmish. 
The Axis forces have fortified their position, anticipating an imminent Allied assault aimed
at crippling their supply lines. Dubbed Operation: Iron Hold, the battle is set to determine
control over crucial war resources.
