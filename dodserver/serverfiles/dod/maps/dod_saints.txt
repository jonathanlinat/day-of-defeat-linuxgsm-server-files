DOD_SAINTS by Arjan "IR" Bak
(arjanb@nuclear-dawn.net)

1943, Sicily

After being pushed out a key village in the hills of sicily, 
the Allies prepare a counterattack; Operation: Saints.
The objectives are simple:

Allied Objectives: 
   - capture the bridge and reclaim the town!

Axis Objectives:
   - Stop the americans from re-taking the bridge and the town 
   
Textures: Philip "Blazeeer" Klevestav , Daniel "kleinluka" Luka,and Arjan "IR" Bak  
Base layout design: Jean-Edouard "kominAaa" Fages
