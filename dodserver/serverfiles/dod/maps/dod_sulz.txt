DOD_SULZ by Christian "DaKurt" (cszb@cszb.net)

January, 1944, Germany

A small ville at the german-french frontier. The Allies are going 
to push the Axis back and capture the city of Sulz.
Due to the snow they are involved in hard fights against the axis 
troups.

Allied Objectives: 
   - Capture the 5 flag points

Axis Objectives:
   - Capture the 5 flag points
