DOD_KALT by Tim "Waldo" Holt (Timh@valvesoftware.com)

December, 1944, Western Front

The half-frozen town of Kalt became the setting for some
of the most brutal fighting of the war - firefights raging 
in the streets, houses, and sewers.

   - Capture the 5 flag points

Axis Objectives:
