June, 1944, Normandy

With the rain stopped, The Allies attempt to hold Sainte Lo as the Germans launch a fierce counter-attack to retake the town. 
Both sides view Sainte Lo as the key intersection leading to larger cities such as Caen and Carentan.

Allied Objectives:
   - Capture the 5 flag points

Axis Objectives: 
   - Capture the 5 flag points
