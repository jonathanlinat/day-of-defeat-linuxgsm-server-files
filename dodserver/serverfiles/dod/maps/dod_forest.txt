August, 1944, Western Front

Two opposing forces have dug-in within the cover of a French forest. Walk carefully and capture the trenches!

Axis Objectives: Capture the 4 trenches. 

Utilize enemy mortars where possible, and use Panzerschrek/Bazooka weapons to take out fortifications.
