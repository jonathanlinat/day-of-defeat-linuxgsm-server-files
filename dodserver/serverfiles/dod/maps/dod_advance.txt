DOD_ADVANCE by Magnar "insta" Jenssen (magnar.jenssen@gmail.com)

Northern France, Near Belgium, Summer 1944

After D-Day the massed Allied armies moved inland through the 
Cotentin peninsula. Axis forces fought a spirited delaying
action and managed to blunt the Allied advance for several
months. In small towns the fighting was brutal house-to-house
combat. This is one of those towns...

Allied Objectives:
   - Capture all 6 points

Axis Objectives:
   - Capture all 6 points
